\section{Discussion}
\label{sec:discussion}

\subsection{What the characterization changes}

The characterization positions state NFCS Big-Five literacy as a cross-sectional asset-pricing moderator operating through an interaction-zone microfoundation, identified through the size $\times$ ownership margin rather than the IV margin the canonical limits-to-arbitrage reading predicts. Where household finance has studied literacy as a predictor of portfolio quality, participation, and resilience \citep{lusardiMitchell2014, calvetCampbellSodini2007, grinblattKeloharjuLinnainmaa2012}, this paper makes state-aggregate literacy a moderator on a specific anomaly interaction with precisely characterized population scope, and adds it to the local-return-predictability program of \citet{korniotisKumar2013} alongside the business-cycle channel --- distinct (identified through IO and size conditioning the business cycle does not predict) but partly overlapping (a business-cycle control block attenuates the literacy three-way 20--23 percent).

Taking the size-controlled mid-IO coefficient $\hat\gamma_{\text{mid-IO}} = -0.0256$, the economic magnitude is a literacy-modulated annualized return differential of about $-5.5\%$ at a $0.187$-standard-deviation swing in the standardized composite; the non-size-controlled coefficient implies $-7\%$, and a classical errors-in-variables correction (reliability ratio $\lambda = 0.420$) extends the bracket to roughly $-13\%$. This $-5.5\%$ to $-13\%$ bracket overlaps the $\approx 5\%$ state-business-cycle strategy of \citet{korniotisKumar2013}.

\subsection{Limitations}

\paragraph{Population scope and sample selection.} On the PIT-corrected full panel the mid-IO coefficient collapses by $\approx 26$ fold, and the relocator characteristics (IA~\ref{ia:relocchar}) lean toward a sample-selection rather than mechanism-strength reading of the collapse: relocators are smaller, higher-IV, lower-IO, lower-return firms, and about one-third of the stable-HQ mid/low gap comes from low-IO attenuation under the relocator drop. The characterization is a statement about firms with stable headquarters across the panel window and does not generalize without modification to the corrected full panel. Pre/post-relocation event studies on the 1{,}366 dropped firms would discriminate between the two readings.

\paragraph{Microfoundation and time variation.} The characterization is positively identified through the size-restriction migration and the cross-state gradient but does not separate the within-family readings (demand intensity, attention, timing) that share the same size $\times$ ownership conditioning; the TAQ-based Boehmer-Jones-Zhang retail trade indicator would separate them. Separately, the pooled cross-state gradient (Holm FWER $p = 0.014$) is driven primarily by post-2015 data; the pre-2015 slope is wrong-signed and concentrated in NFCS Wave 2009. The most likely explanation is measurement noise in the first Big-Five administration rather than a regime change --- institutional ownership rose post-2015 and is uncorrelated with the literacy gradient --- and the within-panel mid-IO coefficient is essentially identical pre- and post-2015, so this is a power issue, not a substantive shift.

\paragraph{Identification: IV, confounds, and functional form.} The K-12 personal-finance mandate instrument is endogenous to state literacy through reverse causality (low-literacy states adopted mandates as a remedy) and fails its first-stage relevance test; the OLS estimate is the preferred inferential statement, and a clean IV would require pre-1990 mandate variation \citep{bernheimGarrettMaki2001} or verifiable-compliance cohort micro-data (IA~\ref{ia:ivfirststage}). The within-stock contrast does not discriminate against an ownership-interactive state-level confound; the canonical instance is state-resident public-pension home-state ownership ($\approx 21$ managers in roughly seven states, collinear with the state fixed effect elsewhere), whose closure requires a CIK-by-CIK state-pension-resident flag not built here. Finally, the continuous-margin quadratic U-shape ($\hat\gamma_5 = +0.0151$, $p = 0.017$) does not survive log-IO, kink-IO, or a flat twenty-bin diagnostic, so the discrete-tercile concentration is the headline functional-form-free characterization and the U-shape is parametric corroboration only.
